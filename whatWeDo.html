  ROOT CANAL TREATMENT / €300 - €600

Root Canal Treatment

Single root (front tooth) €300 - €450, premolar tooth €450, molar tooth €600

Is the treatment painful?

No. With modern anaesthetics and a careful approach, the procedure is painless.

Will my tooth go dark after?

This is rarely an issue, and a bleaching agent can be applied inside the tooth after the treatment if necessary.

Is the treatment guaranteed to work?

The success rate is 90%.

Can the tooth break after?

A root filled tooth is more brittle than an unfilled one. For a side or back tooth, a Porcelain crown is recommended to prevent fracture.

  CROWN / €800

Do the crowns look natural?

Skilled tooth preparation and a good laboratory are essential for a natural look. We use all Porcelain Empress crowns in areas of high cosmetic concern to achieving this look.

Will the crown show dark at the margin after a period?

This is where the all Porcelain crown will minimise the likelihood of an unsightly margin showing. This is not a common problem in our experience.

How long does a crown last?

It is recommended that a crown should be replaced every 10 years. Many last much longer than that.

  BLEACHING / €300

Can I get the “laser” treatment?

You will see many non-dental clinics advertising cheap one visit deals. These are often done without suitability assessments, usually don’t last (if indeed they work at all in the first place) and often produce extreme sensitivity afterwards. Even the dental surgeries that offer this procedure recommend a follow up treatment with the home bleaching trays.

Will the treatment make my teeth sensitive?

This is usually not a problem, but mild sensitivity may occur for the first few days.

Does it last?

We recommend that patients “top-up” by wearing the bleaching tray once a month on an ongoing basis to ensure relapse.

Does it always work?

Suitability assessment is critical. Most patients are suitable, it works for the vast majority of those who are suitable.

Does it stop the teeth staining after the treatment?

Unfortunately not. We recommend routine cleaning visits every 6 months.

  VENEERS / €800

How natural do veneers look?

It is down to skillful tooth preparation and an excellent laboratory, both of which we can provide.

How long do the veneers last?

The average lifetime is 10 years although many last much longer.

Do Veneers break?

This is a rare occurrence which can be corrected by repair or conversion of the veneer into a full crown. Veneers are not recommended for patients who exercise considerable biting force on their teeth. This can be determined at the assessment stage.

  IMPLANTS / €2,800

Is getting the implant painful?

The discomfort of placing the implant is equivalent to a simple extraction. The impression and seating of the crown to the implant generally requires no local anaesthetic.

Do they last?

The success rate for implants is over 90% over a long period of time.

If I am wearing a denture, can I continue to use this while the Implant is beingconstructed?

The patient is not without a tooth at any time during the procedure.
