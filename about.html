About Us and our History!

About Us

The History of my Profession.

I took over the practice in 2000 and we are situated in the center of the village. We are a family orientated practice and pride ourselves in our modern approach to treatment. We have a digitalized x ray system that allows immediate viewing on the computer screen and cuts down on radiation. A full mouth scan using an OPG machine gives a picture of the teeth and supporting structures and can detect gum problems, decay and help with deciding how to treat wisdom teeth.

Prevention is better than cure. We provide a full oral cancer screen at each visit and early treatment of gum problems and fissure sealants as well as early detection of orthodontic issues makes for a lifetime of good dental health. Cosmetic dental treatment is something that people are very interested in and treatments range from bleaching and bonding to veneers, crowns and implants, all of which we can do for you in the surgery. Unfortunately sometimes teeth are lost and in this case implants or a cosmetic well fitted denture is the option.

Patients sometimes are understandably nervous and we do our best to put them at ease. In this case discussing the treatment and talking a soft slow approach usually works. For more difficult procedures such as wisdom teeth extractions, sedation is an option. These are hard financial times for all of us and we are very aware of that. For patients with the appropriate PPS contributions, a check up once a year is free and a check up and clean costs only 40 euro. Even without PPS the cost is 65 euro. This is the only treatment required for a lot of our patients so you can see the yearly out going is small.
