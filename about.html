About Us

The History of my Profession.

I took over the practice in 2000, and since then, I have been dedicated to providing effcient, friendly, and quality service. Located in the centre of Castleknock village, we are a family orientated practice and we pride ourselves in our modern approach to treatment. We have a digitalized x-ray system that allows immediate viewing on the computer screen and cuts down on radiation. A full mouth scan using an OPG machine gives a picture of the teeth and supporting structures. This can detect gum problems, decay and helps with deciding how to treat wisdom teeth.




We believe that prevention is better than cure. At each visit, we provide a full oral cancer screen; early treatment of gum problems and fissure sealants; as well as early detection of orthodontic issues. All of this makes for a lifetime of good dental health. We also provide cosmetic dental treatment including bleaching and bonding, veneers, crowns and implants, and dentures.





Sometimes patients are understandably nervous and we do our best to put them at ease. In this case, discussing the treatment and talking a soft slow approach usually works. Sedation is an option for more difficult procedures, such as wisdom teeth extractions.  For patients with the appropriate PPS contributions, an annual checkup is free, and a checkup and with a clean costs only €15. Without PPS, the cost is €75. This is the only treatment required for a lot of our patients, so as you can see, the costs are generally minimal.
