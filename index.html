ABOUT US

A couple things you should know about us.

  • We have a digitalized x-ray system that allows immediate viewing on the computer screen and cuts down on radiation.
  • For patients with the appropriate PPS, an annual checkup is free, and a checkup with a clean costs only €15.
  • Surgery is open Monday - Friday. However, we can accommodate patients in the evening and at weekends.
  • We see all emergency patients on the same day, and we offer oral sedation for nervous patients.
  • You are promised an efficient, friendly, and quality service.
