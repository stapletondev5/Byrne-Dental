Bleaching / €300

Can I get the “laser” treatment?

You will see many non dental clinics advertising cheap 1 visit deals . These are often done without suitability assessments, usually don’t last ( if indeed they work at all in the first place) and often produce extreme sensitivity afterwards. Even the dental surgeries that offer this procedure recommend a follow up treatment with the home bleaching trays.

Will the treatment make my teeth sensitive?

This is usually not a problem but mild sensitivity may occur for the first few days.

Does it last?

We recommend that patients “top up” by wearing the bleaching tray once a month on an ongoing basis to ensure relapse.

Does it always work?

Suitability assessment is critical . Most patients are suitable and of those patients, it works for the vast majority.

Does it stop the teeth staining after the treatment?

Unfortunately not. We recommend routine cleaning visits every 6 months.

Bridge / €1100 - €2,000

How well does the bridge look?

If the tooth preparation and laboratory work are done to a high standard, the cosmeticsare excellent and match well with the adjacent teeth.

How long does the bridge last?

It is recommended that a bridge should be changed every 10 years although many lastmuch longer than that.

Crown / €800.

Do the crowns look natural?

Skilled tooth preparation and a good laboratory are essential . We use all Porcelain Empress crowns in areas of high cosmetic concern.

Will the crown show dark at the margin after a period?

This is where the all Porcelain crown will minimise the likelihood of an unsightly margin showing. This is not a common problem in our experience.

How long does a crown last?

It is recommended that a crown should be replaced every 10 years. Many last much longer than that.

Veneers / €800

How natural do veneers look?

It is down to skillful tooth preparation and an excellent laboratory both of which we can provide.

How long do the veneers last?

The average lifetime is 10 years although many last much longer.

Do Veneers break?

A rare occurrence which can be corrected by repair or conversion of the veneer in to a full crown. In patients who exercise considerable biting force on their teeth, they are not recommended. This can be determined at the assessment stage.

Root Canal Treatment

Single root (front tooth) €300 to €450, premolar tooth €450, molar tooth €600

Is the treatment painful?

No. With modern anaesthetics and a careful approach, the procedure is painless.

Will my tooth go dark after?

This is rarely an issue and a bleaching agent can be applied inside the tooth after the treatment if necessary.

Is the treatment guaranteed to work?

The success rate is in the high 90%’s.

Can the tooth break after?

A root filled tooth is more brittle than a non filled one. For a side or back tooth, a Porcelain crown is recommended to prevent fracture.
